Free Medicine Website:Your Health Companion

This free medicine website offers valuable health information through three key pages: Home, Healthy Food, and Emergency Treatment.

Home Page: Health News

The Home page provides the latest health news, keeping users updated on medical breakthroughs, wellness tips, and important health alerts.

Healthy Food Page: Nutrition Tips

The Healthy Food page offers advice on foods that boost health. It helps users choose better options for energy, immunity, and overall well-being.

Emergency Treatment Page: First Aid

The Emergency Treatment page guides users on handling urgent situations, such as how to care for someone with heavy bleeding, with clear first aid instructions.
